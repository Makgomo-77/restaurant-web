Our Story

Founded in 1995, Gourmet Delight has been serving exquisite dishes crafted with passion and the finest ingredients. Our chef, with over 25 years of international experience, brings a unique fusion of traditional and modern culinary techniques to your table.

We pride ourselves on sourcing local, organic produce and sustainable seafood to create memorable dining experiences for our guests.

Executive Chef Michael Laurent
